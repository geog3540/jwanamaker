Jayna Wanamaker Undergraduate Student, University of Iowa

Hello, my name is Jayna Wanamaker. I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Geographical and Sustainability Sciences at the University of Iowa. I am majoring in Geography on a Health and Society Track. In addition to my major, I hope to earn a certificate in GIS during my undergraduate years. I plan to graduate in May of 2023. Beyond classes, my current involvement with the University of Iowa includes participation in the Iowa Community Integrated Geography Organization (ICIGO) which is a student organization that that utilizes experiences and skills gained in the classroom to support projects focused on sustainability in the community and state. I am also currently employed by University Housing and Dining as a Student Manager at Catlett Marketplace. When I am not on campus, you are most likely going to find me at one of the following locations: The Campus Recreation and Wellness Center (CRWC) bouldering wall, the Hawkeye Recreation fields for the University of Iowa Women's Ultimate Frisbee club practices (Saucy Nancy), or a few miles in the sky gliding around with a parachute on my back.
