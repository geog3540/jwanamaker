Population Density in Maine

This choropleth map exercise focuses on the state of Maine and its population density based on county.

Graduated Symbol Mapping

A graduated symbol mapping technique was used to map data from the CDC regarding the number of children under the age of 5 in each state. There is a positive relationship between the number of children and circle size.

Crop Loss and Extreme Heat Bivariate (2012)

This map shows the fluctuation between regions in the Midwest receiving indemnity payments and how many extreme heat days they experience. In 2012, a massive heat wave moved over the Midwest, which means we should expect to see high values for both variables.

Flow Mapping Agricultural Interactions

This flow map shows some of the interactions between the Midwest states who have made payments to one another after natural disasters or some agricultural mishap. This information was gathered for the most recent decade from a large number of sources including the USDA. Dollar amounts varied heavily; this flow map is simply looking at the number of times there was an interaction that was found while using the internet.

Final Project - Extreme Heat and Crop Insurance in the Midwest

This small multiples map shows us where these extreme heat days occur, and how they change over the course of our timeframe. This set of maps shows us that there is not a consistent clustering of extreme heat anywhere from year to year. These heat waves tend to hit different locations with varying levels of severity. These results lead us to believe that when there is a correlation between the spatial distribution of extreme heat and crop payments, it is somewhat significant.

ICIGO - Manchester Tree Survey

From May 2021 to March 2022, undergraduate students involved with the Iowa Community Integrated Geography Organization (ICIGO) at the University of Iowa Department of Geographical and Sustainability Sciences worked to create the Manchester Tree Survey. This survey allows interested individuals to view data about trees within the City of Manchester, Iowa.
